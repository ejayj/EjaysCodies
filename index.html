Welcome To Ejay's Codies.xyz!

This is my take on the previously closed site, codies.xyz!

You are a secret agent trying to decrypt a code from the spymaster before your enemy team does. Here's the catch: the spymasters can only give you a one word hint! The wrong word could trigger a bomb or a point for the other team... Can you accomplish this code before the enemy team does theirs?

How To Play:

  1. Get into equal teams, Red or Blue. Each team chooses 1 spymaster.
  2. Spymasters hit the 'Spymaster' button to see their colors.
  3. On your turn, your spymaster can only give a one-word hint.
  4. Players will then choose which word is their answer.
  5. First team who's score to reaches zero wins!

RULES:

  • If you choose the other team's word, your team's turn ends.
  • If you accidentally choose the 'bomb' word, your team loses!
  • Only the spymaster gets to see the colors of the board
  • A team may pass their turn up by hitting the end turn button.

    • Other Options:

    Timer: Activates a time limit of your choosing
    Bomb: This hides or show's the bomb for spymasters.




Check my github for a tutorial on how to use/create this for your friends!
